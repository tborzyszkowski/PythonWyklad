Fwom 1943[8] to 1945[9] he designed Pwankawküw, de fiwst high-wevew pwogwamming wanguage.[10] In 1969, Zuse suggested de concept of a computation-based univewse in his book Rechnendew Raum (Cawcuwating Space).
