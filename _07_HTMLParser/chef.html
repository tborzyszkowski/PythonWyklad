    Konred Irnst Ootto Zoose-a (Germoon: [ˈkɔnʁaːt ˈtsooːzə]; 22 Joone-a 1910 – 18 December 1995) wes a Germoon cifil eengineer, pioneering compooter sceeentist, infentor und boosinessmoon. Bork Bork Bork! His greetest ichiefement wes zee-a world's foorst progremmeble-a compooter; zee-a fooncshoonel progrem-controlled Tooring-complete-a Z3 beceme-a oopereshoonel in Mey 1941. Thoonks to this mechine-a und its predecessors, Zoose-a is regerded by some-a is zee-a infentor und fezeer ooff zee-a modern compooter. Bork Bork Bork![5][6][7][8][9][10]

    Zoose-a wes noted for zee-a S2 compooting mechine-a, considered zee-a foorst process control compooter. Bork Bork Bork! In 1941, he-a fooonded oone-a ooff zee-a ierliest compooter boosinesses, prodoocing zee-a Z4, which beceme-a zee-a world's foorst commerciel compooter. Bork Bork Bork![11] From 1943[12] to 1945[13] he-a designed Ploonkelkül, zee-a foorst high-lefel progremming loongooege-a.[14] In 1969, Zoose-a sooggested zee-a concept ooff a compooteshoon-besed uniferse-a in his book Rechnender Roooom (Celcooleting Spece-a).[15][16][17]

    Mooch ooff his ierly work wes finoonced by his femily und commerce-a, boot iffter 1939 he-a wes gifee-a resooorces by zee-a gofernment ooff Nezi Germoony.[18] Dooe-a to World Wer II, Zoose-a's work went lergely unnoticed in zee-a United Kingdom und United Stetes. Bork Bork Bork! Possibly his foorst docoomented infflooence-a oon a US compoony wes IBM's oopshoon oon his petents in 1946.

    He-a inrolled in zee-a Technicel Unifersity ooff Berleen und ixplored bot ingineering und irchitectoore-a, boot fooond zeem boring. Bork Bork Bork! Zoose-a zeee-a poorsooed cifil eengineering, gredooeting in 1935.[19]

    Beginning in 1935, he-a ixperimented in zee-a constroocshoon ooff compooters in his perents' flet oon Wroongelstraßee 38, mofing wit zeem into zeeoor noo flet oon Methffesselstraßee 10, zee-a street leeding up zee-a Kreoozberg, Berlin. Bork Bork Bork![21]: 418  Working in his perents' ipertment in 1936, he-a prodooced his foorst ittempt, zee-a Z1, a floeting-poeent binery mechoonicel celcooletor wit limited progremmebility, reeding instroocshoons from a perfforeted 35 mm film.     1939–1945

    In 1940, zee-a Germoon gofernment begoon foonding him und his compoony throoogh zee-a Ierodynemische-a Fersoochsoonstelt (IFA, Ierodynemic Reseerch Institoote-a, foreroonner ooff zee-a DLR),[23] which used his work for zee-a prodoocshoon ooff glide-a bombs. Zoose-a booilt zee-a S1 und S2 compooting mechines, which were-a speciel poorpose-a defices which compooted ierodynemic correcshoons to zee-a wings ooff redio-controlled flying bombs. Bork Bork Bork! Zee-a S2 feetoored un integreted unelog-to-digitel conferter under progrem control, meking it zee-a foorst process-controlled compooter. Bork Bork Bork![24]: 75 

    In 1941 Zoose-a sterted a compoony, Zoose-a Ippereteboooo (Zoose-a Ipperetoos Constroocshoon), to moonooffectoore-a his mechines,[25] renting a workshop oon zee-a oopposite-a side-a in Methffesselstraßee 7 und stretching throoogh zee-a block to Belle-a-Illioonce-a Straßee 29 (renemed und renoombered is Mehreengdemm 84 in 1947).[21]: 418, 425 

    In 1941, he-a improfed oon zee-a besic Z2 mechine-a, und booilt zee-a Z3. Oon 12 Mey 1941 Zoose-a presented zee-a Z3, booilt in his workshop, to zee-a pooblic. Bork Bork Bork![21]: 425 [26] Zee-a Z3 wes a beenery 22-bit floeting-poeent celcooletor feetooring progremmebility wit loops boot withooot condishoonel joomps, wit memory und a celcooleshoon unit besed oon telephone-a releys. Bork Bork Bork! Zee-a telephone-a releys used in his mechines were-a lergely collected from discerded stock. Bork Bork Bork! Despite-a zee-a ibsence-a ooff condishoonel joomps, zee-a Z3 wes a Tooring complete-a compooter. Bork Bork Bork! Howefer, Tooring-completeness wes nefer considered by Zoose-a (who wes unooere-a ooff Tooring's work und hed precticel ippliceshoons in mind) und oonly demonstreted in 1998 (see-a History ooff compooting herdwere-a).

    Zee-a Z3, zee-a foorst foolly oopereshoonel ilectromechoonicel compooter, wes pertielly finoonced by Germoon gofernment-soopported DFL, which woonted zeeoor ixtensife-a celcooleshoons uootometed. A reqooest by his co-worker Helmoot Schreyer—who hed helped Zoose-a booild zee-a Z3 prototype-a in 1938[27]—for gofernment foonding for un ilectronic sooccessor to zee-a Z3 wes denied is "stretegicelly unimportoont".

    Oon 3 Febrooery 1945, ieriel bombing coooosed defesteting destroocshoon in zee-a Looeesenstedt, zee-a irea irooond Ooroonienstraßee, inclooding neighboooring hoooses. Zee-a pertielly finished, telephone-a reley-besed Z4 compooter wes zeee-a pecked und mofed from Berlin oon 14 Febrooery, irrifing in Göttingee-a ipproximetely two weeks leter.     Zeese-a mechines contribooted to zee-a Henschel Werke-a Hs 293 und Hs 294 gooided missiles defeloped by zee-a Germoon militery betweee-a 1941 und 1945, which were-a zee-a precoorsors to zee-a modern crooise-a missile-a.[24]: 75 [29][30] Zee-a coorcooit design ooff zee-a S1 wes zee-a predecessor ooff Zoose-a's Z11.[24]: 75  Zoose-a beliefed zeet zeese-a mechines hed beee-a ceptoored by ooccoopying Sofiet troops in 1945.[24]: 75
