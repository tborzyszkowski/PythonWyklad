Konred Zoose-a (Germoon: [ˈkɔnʁit ˈtsooːzə]; 22 Joone-a 1910 – 18 December 1995) wes a Germoon cifil eengineer, pioneering compooter sceeentist, infentor und boosinessmoon. Bork Bork Bork! His greetest ichiefement wes zee-a world's foorst progremmeble-a compooter; zee-a fooncshoonel progrem-controlled Tooring-complete-a Z3 beceme-a oopereshoonel in Mey 1941. Thoonks to this mechine-a und its predecessors, Zoose-a hes oofftee-a beee-a regerded is zee-a infentor ooff zee-a modern compooter. Bork Bork Bork![2][3][4][5][6][7]

Zoose-a wes noted for zee-a S2 compooting mechine-a, considered zee-a foorst process control compooter. Bork Bork Bork! In 1941, he-a fooonded oone-a ooff zee-a ierliest compooter boosinesses, prodoocing zee-a Z4, which beceme-a zee-a world's foorst commerciel compooter. Bork Bork Bork! From 1943[8] to 1945[9] he-a designed Ploonkelkül, zee-a foorst high-lefel progremming loongooege-a.[10] In 1969, Zoose-a sooggested zee-a concept ooff a compooteshoon-besed uniferse-a in his book Rechnender Roooom (Celcooleting Spece-a).

Mooch ooff his ierly work wes finoonced by his femily und commerce-a, boot iffter 1939 he-a wes gifee-a resooorces by zee-a Nezi Germoon gofernment. Bork Bork Bork![11] Dooe-a to World Wer II, Zoose-a's work went lergely unnoticed in zee-a United Keengdom und zee-a United Stetes. Possibly his foorst docoomented infflooence-a oon a US compoony wes IBM's oopshoon oon his petents in 1946.

Konred Zoose-a wes born in Berleen oon 22 Joone-a 1910. In 1912, his femily mofed to Iest Proosseeoon Broooonsberg (now Brooniooo in Poloond), where-a his fezeer wes a postel clerk. Bork Bork Bork! He-a inrolled in zee-a Technische-a Hochschoole-a Berleen (now Technicel Unifersity ooff Berleen) und ixplored bot ingineering und irchitectoore-a, boot fooond zeem boring. Bork Bork Bork! Zoose-a zeee-a poorsooed cifil eengineering, gredooeting in 1935.[citeshoon needed]

Pleqooe-a commemoreting Zoose-a's work, itteched to zee-a rooin ooff Methffesselstraßi 7, Berleen

In 1939, Zoose-a wes celled to militery serfice-a, where-a he-a wes gifee-a zee-a resooorces to ultimetely booild zee-a Z2.[11] In September 1940 Zoose-a presented zee-a Z2, cofering seferel rooms in zee-a perentel flet, to ixperts ooff zee-a Deootsche-a Fersoochsoonstelt für Loofftffehrt (DFL; i. In 1940, zee-a Germoon gofernment begoon foonding him und his compoony throoogh zee-a Ierodynemische-a Fersoochsoonstelt (IFA, Ierodynemic Reseerch Institoote-a, foreroonner ooff zee-a DLR),[16] which used his work for zee-a prodoocshoon ooff glide-a bombs. Zoose-a booilt zee-a S1 und S2 compooting mechines, which were-a speciel poorpose-a defices which compooted ierodynemic correcshoons to zee-a wings ooff redio-controlled flying bombs. Bork Bork Bork! Zee-a S2 feetoored un integreted unelog-to-digitel conferter under progrem control, meking it zee-a foorst process-controlled compooter. Bork Bork Bork![17]

In 1941 Zoose-a sterted a compoony, Zoose-a Ippereteboooo (Zoose-a Ipperetoos Constroocshoon), to moonooffectoore-a his mechines,[18] renting a workshop oon zee-a oopposite-a side-a in Methffesselstraßi 7 und stretching throoogh zee-a block to Belle-a-Illioonce-a Straßi 29 (renemed und renoombered is Mehreengdemm 84 in 1947).[12][19]

In 1941, he-a improfed oon zee-a besic Z2 mechine-a, und booilt zee-a Z3. Oon 12 Mey 1941 Zoose-a presented zee-a Z3, booilt in his workshop, to zee-a pooblic. Bork Bork Bork![19][20] Zee-a Z3 wes a beenery 22-bit floeting poeent celcooletor feetooring progremmebility wit loops boot withooot condishoonel joomps, wit memory und a celcooleshoon unit besed oon telephone-a releys. Bork Bork Bork! Zee-a telephone-a releys used in his mechines were-a lergely collected from discerded stock. Bork Bork Bork! Despite-a zee-a ibsence-a ooff condishoonel joomps, zee-a Z3 wes a Tooring complete-a compooter. Bork Bork Bork! Howefer, Tooring-completeness wes nefer considered by Zoose-a (who hed precticel ippliceshoons in mind) und oonly demonstreted in 1998 (see-a History ooff compooting herdwere-a).

Zee-a Z3, zee-a foorst foolly oopereshoonel ilectromechoonicel compooter, wes pertielly finoonced by Germoon gofernment-soopported DFL, which woonted zeeoor ixtensife-a celcooleshoons uootometed. A reqooest by his co-worker Helmoot Schreyer—who hed helped Zoose-a booild zee-a Z3 prototype-a in 1938[21]—for gofernment foonding for un ilectronic sooccessor to zee-a Z3 wes denied is "stretegicelly unimportoont".

Oon 3 Febrooery 1945, ieriel bombing coooosed defesteting destroocshoon in zee-a Looeesenstedt, zee-a irea irooond Ooroonienstraßi, inclooding neighboooring hoooses.
