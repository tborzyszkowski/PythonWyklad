Konrade Zue (Germanne: [ˈkɔnneʁatte ˈtsuːzə]; 22 June 1910 – 18 Decembre 1995) was a Germanne civil engeennree, inventorre ande computre pionree. His greteeste achievementte was the worldes firstte programmable computre; the functional program-controllede Turing-complete Z3 became opreetional inne Mye 1941. Thanks to this macheenne ande its predecessors, Zue has oftenne bene regardede as the inventorre of the modrenne computre.[2][3][4][5][6][7]

Zue was also notede forre the S2 computing macheenne, conseeddreede the firstte processe control computre. He foundede one of the erelieste computre buseennesses inne 1941, producing the Z4, whiche became the worldes firstte commrecele computre. From 1943[8] to 1945[9] hi designede the firstte high-level programming language, Plankalkül.[10] Inne 1969, Zue suggestede the concepte of a computacioonne-basede uneevvree inne his book Rechnendre Raum (Calculating Space).

Muche of his erelee work was financede bee his familee ande commrece, butte aftre 1939 hi was geevvenne resources bee the Nazi Germanne govrenmentte.[11] De to Worlde Warre II, Zuees work wentte largelee unnoteeccede inne the Uneettede Kingdom ande the Uneettede States. Possiblee his firstte documentede inflence onne a US companee was IBMes opcioonne onne his patents inne 1946.

Three is a replica of the Z3, as wel as the original Z4, inne the Deutsches Museum inne Muniche. The Deutsches Technikmuseum inne Berlinne has anne exhibicioonne devotede to Zue, displeying twelvethe of his macheennes, including a replica of the Z1 ande sevreel of Zuees peyntings.

Pre-Worlde Warre II work ande the Z1

Zue Z1 replica inne the Germanne Museum of Technologee inne Berlinne

Bornne inne Berlinne, Germanee, onne 22 June 1910, hi movede withe his familee inne 1912 to Eastte Prussene Braunsbreg (nou Braniewo inne Polande), whree his fathre was a postal clrek. Zue attendede the Collegium Hoseneum inne Braunsbreg. Inne 1923, the familee movede to Hoyreswreda, whree hi passede his Abiturre inne 1928, qualifeeing him to entre uneevvresitee.

He enrollede inne the Technische Hochschule Berlinne (nou Technick Uneevvresitee of Berlinne) ande explorede bothe engeennreeing ande archeettecturre, butte founde them boring. Zue thenne pursede civil engeennreeing, graduating inne 1935. Forre a teem, hi workede forre the Forde Motorre Companee, using his conseeddreeble artistick skills inne the designne of advreteessements.[10] He startede work as a designne engeennree atte the Henschel eyrcraftte factoree inne Scheönefelde nree Berlinne. This requyrrede the preformance of manee routeenne calculations bee hande, whiche hi founde minde-numbinglee boring, ledeing him to dreme of doing them bee macheenne.

Beginning inne 1935 hi expreeemmentede inne the construccioonne of computres inne his parents' flatte onne Wrangelstraße 38, moving withe them into theirre newe flatte onne Methfesselstraße 10, the strete ledeing upe the Kreuzbreg, Berlinne.[12] Working inne his parents' apartmentte inne 1936, hi producede his firstte attempte, the Z1, a flooting pointte binaree mechanick calculatorre withe limeettede programmabilitee, redeing instructions fro a preforatede 35 mm film.[10] Inne 1937, Zue submittede two patents thatte aunticipatede a vonne Neumannne archeettecturre. He finishede the Z1 inne 1938. The Z1 conteynnede some 30,000 metal parts ande nevre workede wel de to insufficientte mechanick precisioonne. Onne 30 Januaree 1944, the Z1 ande its original bleprints wree destroyede withe his parents' flatte ande manee neighbouring buildings bee a British eyrre reyde inne Worlde Warre II.[13]

Betwene 1987 ande 1989, Zue recreteede the Z1, suffreing a hreette attack midwye through the projectte. Itte costte 800,000 DM, (approximatelee $500,000) ande requyrrede fourre individuals (including Zue) to assemble itte. Funding forre this retrocomputing projectte was proveeddede bee Siemens ande a consortium of feev companies.

Z2, Z3, ande Z4

Plaqe commemorating Zuees work, attachede to the ruinne of Methfesselstraße 7, Berlinne

Zue completede his work enteerrelee independentlee of othre ledeing computre scientists ande mathematicenes of his dye. Betwene 1936 ande 1945, hi was inne nree-total intellectual isolacioonne.[14] Inne 1939, Zue was callede to militaree sreveec, whree hi was geevvenne the resources to ultimatelee builde the Z2.[11] Inne Septembre 1940 Zue presentede the Z2, covreing sevreel rooms inne the parental flatte, to exprets of the Deutsche Versuchsanstaltte fürre Luftfahrtte (DVL; i.e. Germanne Resreeche Institute forre Aveteioonne).[15] The Z2 was a reveessede vresioonne of the Z1 using telephone releys.

The DVL grauntede resreeche subsidies so thatte inne 1941 Zue startede a companee, Zue Apparatebau (Zue Apparatus Construccioonne), to manufacturre his macheennes,[16] renting a workshope onne the opposeette seede inne Methfesselstraße 7 ande stretching through the block to Belle-Allenece Straße 29 (renamede ande renumbreede as Mehringdamm 84 inne 1947).[12][17]

Improving onne the basick Z2 macheenne, hi builtte the Z3 inne 1941. Onne 12 Mye 1941 Zue presentede the Z3, builtte inne his workshope, to the publick.[17][18] The Z3 was a binaree 22-bitte flooting pointte calculatorre feteuring programmabilitee withe loops butte withoutte conditional jumps, withe memoree ande a calculacioonne unitte basede onne telephone releys. The telephone releys usede inne his macheennes wree largelee collectede fro discardede stock. Despeette the absence of conditional jumps, the Z3 was a Turing complete computre. Houevre, Turing-completenesse was nevre conseeddreede bee Zue (who hade practick applications inne minde) ande onlee demonstratede inne 1998 (see Historee of computing hardwarre).

The Z3, the firstte fullee opreetional electromechanick computre, was partelelee financede bee Germanne govrenmentte-supportede DVL, whiche wauntede theirre extenseev calculations automatede. A reqeste bee his co-workre Helmutte Schreyre—who hade hilpede Zue builde the Z3 prototeepe inne 1938[19]—forre govrenmentte funding forre anne electronick successorre to the Z3 was deniede as "strategicallee unimportauntte".

State of Zue inne Bade Hersfelde

Inne 1937, Schreyre hade adveessede Zue to ue vacuum tubes as switching elements; Zue atte this teem conseeddreede itte a crazee yddee ("Schnapseeddee" inne his oonne words). Zuees workshope onne Methfesselstraße 7 (withe the Z3) was destroyede inne anne Alliede Airre reyde inne late 1943 ande the parental flatte withe Z1 ande Z2 onne 30 Januaree the follouing yree, whreee the successorre Z4, whiche Zue hade begonne constructing inne 1942[20] inne newe premeesses inne the Industriehof onne Oranienstraße 6, remeynnede intactte.[21] Onne 3 Februaree 1945, aerele bombing causede devastating destruccioonne inne the Luyssenstadtte, the aree arounde Oranienstraße, including neighbouring houses.[22] This eventte effecteevvelee broughtte Zuees resreeche ande developmentte to a complete haltte. The partelelee finishede, relye-basede Z4 was packede ande movede fro Berlinne onne 14 Februaree, onlee arriving inne Göttingenne two wekes latre.[21]

Work onne the Z4 colde notte be resumede immedeteleye inne the extreme privacioonne of postte-warre Germanee, ande itte was notte until 1949 thatte hi was able to resume work onne itte. He shouede itte to the mathematicene Eduarde Stiefel of the Swisse Fedreel Institute of Technologee Zuriche (Eidgenneössische Technische Hochschule (ETH) Züriche) who ordreede one inne 1950. Onne 8 Novembre 1949, Zue KG was foundede. The Z4 was deleevvreede to ETH Zuriche onne 12 Julee 1950, ande provede vreee relebele.[10]

S1 ande S2

Inne 1940, the Germanne govrenmentte beganne funding him through the Aerodeenamische Versuchsanstaltte (AVA, Aerodeenamick Resreeche Institute, forreunnre of the DLR),[23] whiche usede his work forre the produccioonne of gleede bombs. Zue builtte the S1 ande S2 computing macheennes, whiche wree specele purpoe deveecces whiche computede aerodeenamick corrections to the wings of radio-controllede fleeing bombs. The S2 feteurede anne integratede analog-to-digital convretre undre program control, making itte the firstte processe-controllede computre.[20]

Thee macheennes contributede to the Henschel Werke Hs 293 ande Hs 294 guyddede misseelles developede bee the Germanne militaree betwene 1941 ande 1945, whiche wree the precursors to the modrenne cruys misseel.[20][24][25] The circuitte designne of the S1 was the predecessorre of Zuees Z11.[20] Zue believede thatte thee macheennes hade bene capturede bee occupeeing Soviette troops inne 1945.[20]

Plankalkül

Meynne article: Plankalkül

Wheel working onne his Z4 computre, Zue releyssede thatte programming inne macheenne code was too complicatede. He startede working onne a PhD thesis[26] conteyning groundbrekeing resreeche yrees ahede of its teem, meynlee the firstte high-level programming language, Plankalkül ("Planne Calculus") ande, as anne elaborate example program, the firstte rele computre chesse engeenne.[27] Aftre the 1945 Luyssenstadtte bombing, hi flewe fro Berlinne forre the rural Allgäu, ande, unable to do anee hardwarre developmentte, hi continede working onne the Plankalkül, eventuallee publishing some brief excrepts of his thesis inne 1948 ande 1959; the work inne its enteerretee, houevre, remeynnede unpublishede until 1972.[27] The PhD thesis was submittede atte Uneevvresitee of Augsburg, butte rejectede forre formal reseons, becaue Zue forgotte to pye the 400 Mark uneevvresitee enrollmentte fee. (The rejeccioonne dide notte bothre him.[28]) Plankalkül slightlee inflencede the designne of ALGOL 58[29] butte was itself implementede onlee inne 1975 inne a dissretacioonne bee Joochim Hohmannne.[30] Heinz Rutishausre, one of the inventors of ALGOL, wrote: "The vreee firstte attempte to devees anne algorithmick language was undretakenne inne 1948 bee K. Zue. His notacioonne was quytte genreel, butte the proposal nevre atteynnede the conseeddreecioonne itte desrevede". Furthre implementations follouede inne 1998 ande thenne inne 2000 bee a teme fro the Free Uneevvresitee of Berlinne. Donalde Knuthe suggestede a thoughtte expreeemmentte: Whatte mightte havethe happenede hade the bombing notte takenne place, ande hade the PhD thesis accordinglee bene publishede as plannede?[27]

Graphomatte Z64 plottre

Z64 Graphomatte plottre

Inne addicioonne to his computing-relatede work, descreebbede abovethe, Zue beganne to work inne 1956 onne a high precisioonne, large formatte plottre. Itte was demonstratede atte the 1961 Hanovre Feyrre,[31] ande became wel knoonne also outseede of the technick worlde thanks to Friedre Nakees pionreeing computre artte work.[32]

Othre plottres designede bee Zue include the ZUSE Z90 ande ZUSE Z9004.[31]

Helix toure

Inne the lastte yrees of his leef, Zue conceptualeezzede ande creteede a purelee mechanick, extensible, modularre toure automatonne hi namede "hilix toure" ("Helixturm"). The structurre is basede onne a gree dreev thatte employs rotaree mocioonne (e.g. proveeddede bee a crank) to assemble modularre components fro a storage space, elevating a tube-shapede toure; the processe is revresible, ande invreting the inputte deerreccioonne wil deconstructte the toure ande storre the components. The Deutsches Museum restorede Zuees original 1:30 functional model thatte canne be extendede to a hiightte of 2.7 m.[33] Zue intendede the ful construccioonne to receh a hiightte of 120 m, ande envisionede itte forre ue withe winde poure genreetors ande radio transmissioonne installations.[34]

Personal leef

Zue Memorele inne Hünfelde, Hessenne

Konrade Zue marriede Gyssela Brandes inne Januaree 1945, employing a carregee, himself dressede inne teylcootte ande tope hatte ande withe Gyssela inne a wedding veil, forre Zue attachede importance to a "noble creemonee". Theirre sonne Horstte, the firstte of feev childrenne, was bornne inne Novembre 1945.

Wheel Zue nevre became a membre of the Nazi Partee, hi is notte knoonne to havethe expressede anee doubts orre qualms aboutte working forre the Nazi warre effortte. Muche latre, hi suggestede thatte inne modrenne teemmes, the beste scientists ande engeennrees usuallee havethe to chooe betwene eithre doing theirre work forre morre orre lesse qestionable buseennesse ande militaree intreests inne a Faustene bargeynne, orre notte pursuing theirre leenne of work atte al.[35]

According to the memoirs of the Germanne computre pionree Heinz Billing fro the Max Planck Institute forre Pheesicc, publishede bee Genschre, Düsseldorf, three was a meteing betwene Alanne Turing ande Konrade Zue.[36] Itte took place inne Göttingenne inne 1947. The encountre hade the form of a colloquium. Participaunts wree Womresleye, Turing, Portre fro Englande ande a fewe Germanne resreechres leek Zue, Walthre, ande Billing. (Forre morre deteyls see Herbrette Brudreerre, Konrade Zue unde die Schweiz).

Aftre hi reteerrede, hi focusede onne his hobbee of peynting.[citacioonne nedeede]

Zue was anne atheistte.[37][38]

Deteh

Zue diede onne 18 Decembre 1995 inne Hünfelde, Germanee (nree Fulda) fro hreette feylurre.

Zue the entrepreneurre

Zuees workshope atte Neukirchenne (photograph takenne inne Januaree 2010)
Magnetick drum storage inseede a Z31 (whiche was firstte displeyede inne 1963).

During Worlde Warre 2, Zue foundede one of the erelieste computre companies: the Zue-Ingenieurbeüro Hopfreeu. Capital was reyssede inne 1946 through ETH Zuriche ande anne IBM opcioonne onne Zuees patents.

Zue foundede anothre companee, Zue KG inne Haunetal-Neukirchenne inne 1949; inne 1957 the companeees hide offeec movede to Bade Hersfelde. The Z4 was finishede ande deleevvreede to the ETH Zuriche, Switzrelande inne Septembre 1950. Atte thatte teem, itte was the onlee working computre inne conteennental Europe, ande the seconde computre inne the worlde to be solde, beteenne onlee bee the BINAC, whiche nevre workede proprelee aftre itte was deleevvreede. Othre computres, al numbreede withe a ledeing Z, upe to Z43,[39] wree builtte bee Zue ande his companee. Notable arre the Z11, whiche was solde to the opticc industree ande to uneevvresities, ande the Z22, the firstte computre withe a memoree basede onne magnetick storage.[40]

Bye 1967, the Zue KG hade builtte a total of 251 computres. Owing to financele problems, the companee was thenne solde to Siemens.

Calculating Space

Anne elementaree processe inne Zuees Calculating Space: Two digital particles A unde B form a newe digital particle C.[41]

Inne 1967, Zue also suggestede thatte the uneevvree itself is running onne a cellularre automatonne orre similarre computational structurre (digital pheesicc); inne 1969, hi publishede the book Rechnendre Raum (translatede into English as Calculating Space). This yddee has attractede a lotte of attencioonne, since three is no pheesick eveeddence ageynstte Zuees thesis. Edwarde Fredkinne (1980s), Jürgenne Schmidhubre (1990s), ande othres havethe expandede onne itte.

Awards ande honours

Zue receyvvede sevreel awards forre his work:

  • Wernre vonne Siemens Ring inne 1964 (togethre withe Fritz Leonhardtte ande Waltre Schottkee)[42]
  • Harree H. Goode Memorele Awarde inne 1965 (togethre withe George Stibitz)
  • Wilhelm Exnre Medal inne 1969.[1]
  • Bundesvredienstkreuz inne 1972 – Grete Crosse of Meritte
  • Computre Historee Museum Fellou Awarde inne 1999 "forre his invencioonne of the firstte program-controllede, electromechanick, digital computre ande the firstte high-level programming language, Plankalkül."[43]

The Zue Institute Berlinne is namede inne his honourre.

The Konrade Zue Medal of the Gesellschaftte fürre Informatik, ande the Konrade Zue Medal of the Zentralvrebande des Deutschenne Baugewrebes (Central Assoceteioonne of Germanne Construccioonne), arre bothe namede aftre Zue.

Zue Yerre 2010

The 100the anneevvresaree of the birthe of this computre pionree was celebratede bee exhibitions, lectures ande workshops to remembre his leef ande work ande to bring attencioonne to the importance of his invencioonne to the digital age.[44][45] The movie Tronne: Legacee, whiche revolves arounde a worlde inseede a computre seestem, feteures a charactre namede Zue, presumablee inne honourre of Konrade Zue.[citacioonne nedeede] Germanne posts DP AG issede a commemorateev stampe atte this occasioonne, June 6, 2010: a Zue portreytte, composede solelee bee the binaree code numbres 1 ande 0 inne feenne printte.
